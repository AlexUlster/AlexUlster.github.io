## Project Development Process

My approach to game development follows an iterative design methodology:

1. **Concept & Prototyping** - Rapid iteration to validate core gameplay
2. **Pre-Production** - Documentation, asset planning, technical design
3. **Production** - Full development cycle with regular playtesting
4. **Polish & Iteration** - Feedback implementation and quality assurance
5. **Launch & Support** - Release preparation and post-launch refinement
