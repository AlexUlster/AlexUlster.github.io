## Welcome

I'm Alex Dickie, a game designer and developer passionate about creating engaging player experiences through thoughtful design and technical implementation.

## Background

With a strong foundation in game design principles and development practices, I've worked on multiple projects ranging from game jam prototypes to full-scale productions. My approach combines creative vision with technical expertise to deliver polished, player-focused experiences.

- **Project Management**: Agile methodologies, team leadership, scope management
- **Collaboration**: Cross-functional teamwork, communication, documentation

### Tools & Technologies

- C# Programming
- Git & Version Control

## Philosophy

I believe that great games come from understanding the player first. Every design decision should enhance the player experience, whether through intuitive controls, meaningful feedback, or engaging narratives.
